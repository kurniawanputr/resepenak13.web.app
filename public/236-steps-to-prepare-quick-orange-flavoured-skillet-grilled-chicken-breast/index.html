The ingredients needed to make Orange flavoured skillet grilled chicken breast:

Steps to make Orange flavoured skillet grilled chicken breast:
  1. Pat dry the chicken. Apply the vegetable oil on both sides of the chicken. Sprinkle salt and pepper and leave to marinate for 3 hours or so.
  2. In a bowl add the soy sauce, orange juice and honey and mix
  3. In a greased hot skillet (i used a cast iron skilley) place the chicken breast and let them cook on both sides carefull not to burn
  4. Take the paste and apply on the chicken, both sides while still grilling.
  5. Serve with fries or mashed potatoes and a cold drink 😊
