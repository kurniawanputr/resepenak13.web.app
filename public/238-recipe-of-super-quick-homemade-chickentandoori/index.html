The ingredients needed to make ChickenTandoori:

  2. Take 1 cup hung curd
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp roasted besan
  4. Take 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  5. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Take 1 tsp garammasala powder

  9. Take 2 tsp tandoori masala powder
