The ingredients needed to make Chicken Hasselback:
  1. Prepare 3 pcs chicken breast
  2. Get 3 spoons soya sauce
  3. Get 3 spoons ginger & garlic paste
  4. Get 3 spoons tomato sauce
  5. Take 2 spoons red chilli powder
  6. Make ready 1 bowl flour
  7. Take 1 bowl breadcrumb
  8. Prepare 2 beaten egg
  9. Prepare As per taste Salt
  10. Take As required Oil for frying

Steps to make Chicken Hasselback:
  1. Cut the chicken breast in a slice, keeping the base.
  2. Then marinate the chicken with soya sauce, ginger garlic paste, tomato sauce, salt and red chilli powder.
  3. Then in a pan heat the oil. Next wrap the chicken in a flour.
  4. Then wrap it in a beaten egg, then in a breadcrumb.
  5. Next deep fry in a low flame. Chicken Hasselback ready to serve.
