Steps to make Beef Biryani Curry with Pawpaw:
  1. BEEF MARINATION :. In a cooking pot, place the meat chopped into preferable sizes, pour in the garlic, ginger, tomatoes, Pilau mix (table spoon), Tomato paste, Pawpaw. Mix evenly, cover and let it sit up to about 3 hours.
  2. FOR THE RICE: •3 cups of rice - - Vegetable oil (table spn) - - Cummin seeds (1/2 tea spn) - - Food colour (orange) - - Onion (1, cubbed)
  3. Wash the rice approximately thrice and strain it. Heat oil in a pan, add in the cummin spice and onions, fry until golden brown. Measure 3 and 1/2 cups of warm water and add into the oil with 1/2 a tea spn of salt. As the mixture boils, add the rice and let it boil. As water goes down, adjust the heat to low, mix some food colour with a table spoon of water and pour onto about 4 different sides to have the rice in both white and orange color.
  4. Cover and let it simmer until it's ready with top part forming a crust and some moisture retained within.
