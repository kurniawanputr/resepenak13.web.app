The ingredients needed to make Nan's Beef & Noodles:
  1. Take 3 T. Olive oil
  2. Make ready 1 lg. Onion, diced
  3. Take 1 lb. Stew beef (see Note above)
  4. Take 5 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Take 1/2 tsp. Thyme
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp. Rosemary
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp. Pepper
  8. Prepare 1/3 c. Red wine vinegar
  9. Prepare 32 oz. box Beef broth
  10. Prepare 32 oz. box Chicken broth
  11. Prepare 1 c. hot water
  12. Take 1 tsp. Better Than Bouillon base, beef
  13. Prepare 1 tsp. Better Than Bouillon base, chicken
  14. Take 2 pkgs. Reames frozen noodles

Steps to make Nan's Beef & Noodles:
  1. In large soup pot, heat oil over medium to medium-high heat. Add the onions and beef and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in minced garlic and continue cooking 5 more minutes.
  2. Stir in the seasonings and red wine vinegar. Cook 1 minute more.
  3. Stir the Better Than Bouillon bases into the hot water. Add to the pot, along with the 2 boxes of broth. Increase heat and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.
  4. When boiling, reduce heat to lowest temperature and cover the pot. Cook on low for 2 hours, stirring every 20 minutes.
  5. Add Reames frozen noodles, stir, cover the pot and continue cooking on low another 1 hour and 45 minutes, stirring every 15-20 minutes.
  6. Serve. This goes well with buttered crusty bread!
