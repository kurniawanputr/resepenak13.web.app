The ingredients needed to make Chicken Teriyaki チキン照り焼き:
  1. Get 1 Chicken Thigh (deboned, with / without skin)
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp Japanese Soy Sauce
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp Sake
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Granulated Sugar
  5. Make ready Slices Broccoli Florets, Pumpkin

Instructions to make Chicken Teriyaki チキン照り焼き:
  1. Heat the frying pan with adequate of oil, skin down (if with skin), pan fried until brownish (around 3 minutes), turn to the other side and continue to pan fry until brownish
  2. Pour the pre mix seasoning mixture (becareful because the temperature is high and the seasoning is with sake) and let the chicken absorb the seasoning.
  3. Turn to the other when you see the seasoning starting to coat on the chicken. Dish up when the gravy is reducing and thicker.
  4. Served with a bowl of rice and blanced Broccoli florets and pumpkin slices.
