The ingredients needed to make Beef and Mini-Dumplings:
  1. Take Beef and gravy
  2. Make ready 3 pound beef sirloin
  3. Get 48 ounces beef broth divided
  4. Take 1/4 cup chopped chives
  5. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. Prepare 1 ounces envelope aujus mix
  8. Prepare Mini-Dumplings
  9. Take 1 cup all purpose flour unbleached
  10. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
  11. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper powder

Instructions to make Beef and Mini-Dumplings:
  1. Slice the beef into bite sized cubes. Add salt and pepper.
  2. Add your aujus mix and stir the beef in a heavy pot. I used a cast iron Dutch oven.
  3. Add the chives and 3 cups of beef broth simmer 25 minutes.
  4. Mix some of the beef broth with the flour, white pepper, and salt. Get it pourable.
  5. As the meat is simmering pour in the flour mixture stirring. You want to make a huge amount of lumps.
  6. Add the rest of the broth. After it simmered for 12 minutes serve with some rice, noodles or cheesy mashed potatoes. I hope you enjoy!!!!!!!!
