The ingredients needed to make Beef Ramen with Black Forest Ham and Chives:
  1. Make ready 1 package beef ramen
  2. Make ready 3 slices black forest ham
  3. Make ready 2 cups hot water
  4. Take 1 teaspoon soya sauce
  5. Take 1 teaspoon Chinese black vinegar
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons chopped chives

Steps to make Beef Ramen with Black Forest Ham and Chives:
