The ingredients needed to make Chicken Alfredo with Grilled Apples:
  1. Prepare 4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (we only had 2)
  2. Take 1-2 large Braeburn or Gala apple, cut into 1/2-inch wedges
  3. Get 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  4. Get Alfredo sauce, warmed (we used 2 jars of our favorite sauce)
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese (for the top garnish when making plates or bowls) or can use shredded Parmesan (we used parmesan for the kids)
  6. Take Can mushrooms
  7. Make ready Fresh spinach
  8. Make ready Penne or Fettuccine or spaghetti whatever you have
  9. Take Shredded Parmesan for the Alfredo sauce optional
