Instructions to make Healthy chicken breast (oven toaster):
  1. Marinate chicken breast in soy sauce, sesame oil, & honey for a minimum of one hour.
  2. Create a container using foil and coat the inside area with cooking oil
  3. Place the chicken breast on the aluminum foil and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, & oregano. Heat it in the oven toaster for 7-8 mins. Flip it afterwards and cook for another 7-8 mins or until the inside is cooked.
