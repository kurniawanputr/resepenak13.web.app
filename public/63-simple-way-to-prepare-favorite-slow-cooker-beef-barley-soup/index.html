The ingredients needed to make Slow Cooker Beef barley soup:
  1. Get 1.5 lbs chuck roast
  2. Take 1.5 lbs potatoes peeled and cubed 1/2 inch
  3. Get 2 carrots peeled and cubed
  4. Get 1 onion diced
  5. Make ready 4 cloves garlic
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp tomato paste
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp better than bouillion
  8. Prepare 2 tsp Worcester sauce
  9. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Take 1/4 tsp pepper
  11. Prepare 4 sprigs fresh thyme
  12. Take 2 bay leaf
  13. Prepare 6 cups beef broth
  14. Take 2/3 cup barley
  15. Take Option - mushrooms or other vegetables
  16. Get oil of choice
