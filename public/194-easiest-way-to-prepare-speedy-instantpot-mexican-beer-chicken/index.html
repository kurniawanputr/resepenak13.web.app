The ingredients needed to make InstantPot Mexican Beer Chicken:
  1. Take 2 lbs Chicken breast
  2. Get 1/2 bottle beer
  3. Make ready 1 pkg taco seasoning
  4. Make ready Salt and pepper
  5. Make ready 3 Tbsp minced garlic
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup pickled Jalepenos (optional)

Instructions to make InstantPot Mexican Beer Chicken:
  1. Cut chicken breast in half (fillet them) and place them in instantpot
  2. Add seasoning, garlic, beer, and Jalepenos
  3. Place lid on and make sure it's on "sealed". Seat on high heat for 13 minutes. Once done, let it naturally release for 5 minutes and then do a quick release. Shred chicken and serve on tortillas
