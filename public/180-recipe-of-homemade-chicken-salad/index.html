Steps to make Chicken Salad:
  1. By the side of a plate, make a bed with lettuce.
  2. On it, put some diced boiled carrot (boiled for 5 mins)
  3. On top of the carrot, put some finely chopped red pimento
  4. On top of the red pimento, put some finely shredded lettuce.
  5. On it add some boiled carrot
  6. Repeat process till u get your desired quantity.
  7. On it, put some red pimento
  8. By the other side of the plate, put some chopped breast chicken. (Was boiled in salted water)
  9. On the chicken, put some mayonnaise as dressing.
  10. On the mayonnaise, sprinkle some red pimento.
  11. By the side, garnish with sliced hard boiled egg and tomatoes.
  12. Yummy, my chicken salad is ready 😋
