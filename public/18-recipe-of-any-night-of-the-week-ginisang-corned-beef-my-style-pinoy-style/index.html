The ingredients needed to make Ginisang Corned Beef | My Style | Pinoy Style:
  1. Prepare Canned Corned beef
  2. Take Chopped tomatoes (4pcs)
  3. Take Minced garlic (2 clove)
  4. Take Chopped red onion
  5. Make ready Magic Sarap (salt replacement)
  6. Make ready Pepper
  7. Take 1/4 cup water
  8. Make ready Cooking oil
