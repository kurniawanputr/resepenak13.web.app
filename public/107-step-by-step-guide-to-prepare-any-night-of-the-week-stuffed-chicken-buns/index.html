The ingredients needed to make Stuffed chicken buns:
  1. Prepare 250 gm flour
  2. Get 100 gm butter
  3. Take 1 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  4. Get 1 cup milk
  5. Take 1 tbsp baking soda
  6. Make ready 1 cup shredded chicken
  7. Take 1 cup sliced onion
  8. Make ready 1 cup sliced capsicum(green)
  9. Prepare Handful mushrooms
  10. Get As needed Cooking oil
  11. Take as needed Peppercorns,salt

Instructions to make Stuffed chicken buns:
  1. Mix the flour with the butter and baking powder, baking soda, salt, add on warm milk to make dough preferably on dough mixer to keep the consistency fine
  2. Cover the mixture and keep it for 10min
  3. For the stuffing add chicken,onions,capsicum and shallow fry it while adding salt and pepper
  4. Take the dough mix and roll it evenly to small balls then taking one ball at a time flatten it and put some stuffing and then roll the sides up
  5. Brush all the buns with melted butter and sprinkle some sesame seeds/kaalajeera on top
  6. Pre heat oven to 375 celsius and keep the buns for 35-40mins
  7. Serve it hot
