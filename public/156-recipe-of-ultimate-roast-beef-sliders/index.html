Steps to make Roast Beef Sliders:
  1. Preheat oven to 350° and cut rolls in half.
  2. Melt butter, mix ingredients to make sauce and bring to a boil.
  3. Spread sauce on the split bottom rolls and layer cheese. Place in oven for 12 to 15 minutes until cheese is melting. Add roast beef, more sauce on roast beef, add top split roll. Pour the rest of sauce on top of rolls.
  4. Add tooth picks, cover with aluminum foil and place in the oven for 35 to 40 mins.
  5. Take out of oven, remove foil and place back in oven for 10 to 15 more minutes. Let sit for 15 minutes before cutting and serving.
