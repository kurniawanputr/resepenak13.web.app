The ingredients needed to make Bulgogi (Korean beef BBQ):
  1. Get 600 g Beef sliced rib eye or sirloin steak
  2. Prepare 1/2 Onion
  3. Make ready Marinated Ingredients
  4. Prepare 6 tbsp Soy sauce
  5. Make ready 3 tsp Sake(White dry wine)
  6. Take 3 tbsp Sugar
  7. Take 1 tbsp Sessame oil
  8. Take 1/2 Gouchjang
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Grated garlic
  10. Take 1/2 tsp Grated ginger
  11. Get 1/2 Apple
  12. Prepare 1/2 Onion

Steps to make Bulgogi (Korean beef BBQ):
  1. Thinly slice the beef.
  2. Halve the onion, thinly slice 1/2 of the onion and chop the rest.
  3. Chop the apple.
  4. Put onion, apple, garlic, ginger in the food processor and blend until smooth. Put this in a large bowl.
  5. Add the soy sauce, sake, sugar, sesame oil and gochujang in the same bowl.
  6. Add beef and onion in the bowl.
  7. Mix well.
  8. Keep in the fridge overnight to 24 hours.
  9. Heat the frying pan over medium-high heat.
  10. Put sesame oil in the frying pan.
  11. Place the beef one by one in the pan.
  12. Cook until golden-brown for 1-2 minutes and then turn over.
  13. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until both sides are golden-brown.
