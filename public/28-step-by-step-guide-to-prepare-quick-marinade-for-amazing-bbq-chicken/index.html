The ingredients needed to make Marinade for AMAZING BBQ Chicken:
  1. Take 1 Small onion - finely diced
  2. Prepare 1 tsp Pink salt
  3. Prepare T tsp Mixed peppercorns
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Szechuan pepper
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Celery seeds
  6. Get 1 tsp Crushed Garlic
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup Olive Oil
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup Lime Juice
  9. Take 2 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  10. Take 1 Tbsp Dried Sage
  11. Make ready 1 tsp dried Thyme
  12. Prepare 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  13. Get 4 Full Chicken legs (4 thighs & 4 drums)
