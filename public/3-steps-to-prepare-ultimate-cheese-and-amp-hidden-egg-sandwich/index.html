Steps to make Cheese & Hidden Egg Sandwich ü•™:
  1. Take 1 slice of bread & add cheese & cover with another slice of bread.
  2. Make a hole in the center of the bread sandwich with the help of a round cutter or cup & keep it aside.
  3. Heat a pan with ghee. Put the bread sandwich & pour egg in the center hole gently. Cover the egg center whole with the cut bread piece. Apply some ghee on top. Cook it on low flame.
  4. Flip the sandwich to other side once it is cooked. Cook both sides.
  5. Sandwich is ready when the egg is settled and cooked. (We can half cook depends on how you want it) Sprinkle salt, black pepper & red chilli powder.
