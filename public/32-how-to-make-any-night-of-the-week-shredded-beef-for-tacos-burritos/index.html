The ingredients needed to make Shredded Beef for Tacos/Burritos:
  1. Take 1/2 kg beef (I used beef forequarter)
  2. Take 2 tbsp cooking oil
  3. Prepare To taste salt and pepper
  4. Get 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp chilli powder
  6. Get 1 tbsp ground coriander seed
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp ground cumin seed
  8. Prepare 2 bay leaves
  9. Take 2 cinnamon sticks
  10. Get 1 tsp paprika
  11. Make ready 4 tbsp lime juice
  12. Take 1 cup white wine
  13. Get 1 cup water

Instructions to make Shredded Beef for Tacos/Burritos:
  1. Season beef generously with salt and pepper.
  2. In a medium pot, heat oil on medium heat and brown the beef on all sides, about eight minutes.
  3. Add all the dry spices, wine, water, and lime juice. Bring to a boil. Lower the heat to low and let simmer for 2–3 hours or until the meat is tender enough to shred.
  4. Shred the beef with two forks.
  5. Serve wrapped in a tortilla along with favourite taco/burrito fillings.
