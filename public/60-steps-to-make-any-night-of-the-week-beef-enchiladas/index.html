The ingredients needed to make Beef Enchiladas:
  1. Make ready 1 lb ground beef
  2. Prepare 1 Medium onion diced
  3. Prepare 2 cans red enchilada sauce
  4. Get 8 flour tortillas
  5. Prepare 3 cups shredded Mexican cheese

Instructions to make Beef Enchiladas:
  1. Brown ground beef and onions seasoned with salt and pepper and drain.
  2. Put 1/2 can of enchilada sauce in the bottom of a 13 X 9 baking dish.
  3. Mix the other half of enchilada sauce with ground beef mixture and add 1 1/2 cups shredded Mexican cheese.
  4. Place small amount of ground beef mixture in each tortilla shell and roll up. Place each one in the baking dish
  5. Top with 1 can red enchilada sauce and the remaining cheese.
  6. Bake uncovered at 350° for 20 minutes. Garnish with diced tomatoes, lettuce and sour cream
