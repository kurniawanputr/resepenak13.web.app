The ingredients needed to make Beef sauce:
  1. Get 1/2 cup beef broth
  2. Take 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  3. Take 1 tablespoon butter
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  6. Make ready 1 cloves garlic sliced
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar unfiltered
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon sugar
  9. Prepare 1 tablespoon extra Virgin olive oil

Steps to make Beef sauce:
  1. Heat the oil add the pepper when you smell the pepper add the garlic and allow to fry but not burn.
  2. Add the tomato paste, sugar, vinegar, butter, salt, and beef broth.
  3. Stir well till it's incorporated.
  4. Simmer 7 minutes.
  5. Your sauce is ready.
