The ingredients needed to make Winter Beef Stew:
  1. Prepare 1.5 lbs beef stew meat
  2. Get 1.5 lbs potatoes
  3. Get 6 cut up carrots
  4. Get 1 onion chopped
  5. Get 3 celery ribs chopped
  6. Prepare 4 tbsp flour
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp Olive oil
  8. Take 1 can diced tomatoes
  9. Prepare 1 can beef broth
  10. Prepare Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Winter Beef Stew:
  1. Put potatoes, carrots, onion and celery in crockpot. Brown meat using flour and olive oil. Put over vegetables.
  2. Mix broth, tomatoes salt and pepper. Pour over meat.
  3. Cook on low for 8 hours on low.
