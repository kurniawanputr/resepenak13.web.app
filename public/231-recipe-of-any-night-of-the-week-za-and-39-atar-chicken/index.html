The ingredients needed to make Za'atar Chicken:
  1. Prepare 500 gr skinless, boneless chicken breast
  2. Get 3 tbsp za'atar
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  4. Get 1 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp lemon juice
  6. Get 1/2 tsp sugar or honey
  7. Prepare Oil for frying (about 2-3 tbsp)

Steps to make Za'atar Chicken:
  1. Cut and slice chicken breast to your preference. Use a fork to create little holes on the chicken.
  2. Mix the rest of the ingredients (except oil for frying) and rub it to the chicken. Marinate overnight in the fridge.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. I use medium heat. Pan-fry both sides until the chicken is properly cooked.
