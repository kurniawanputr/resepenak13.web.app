The ingredients needed to make Beef Patties:
  1. Make ready Patty
  2. Prepare 2 pound ground chuck
  3. Prepare To taste kosher salt
  4. Make ready To taste ground black pepper
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  6. Get Topping
  7. Take 1/2 large onion coarsely diced
  8. Get 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
  9. Take 4 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  10. Prepare Additionally
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup water for steam melting cheeses

Steps to make Beef Patties:
  1. Heat a pan. Form the ground chuck into 4 patties. Sear them in a pan. Season the top of each patty.
  2. After it has seared for 12 minutes covered, turn them over and season with salt, pepper, and garlic. Top with the onion. Cover for 12 minutes.
  3. Add the cheeses. Cover and cook adding water for 8 minutes.
  4. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!
