The ingredients needed to make Chicken and bacon stuffed croissants:
  1. Take 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
  2. Get 2 regular size cans of Pillsbury crescent rolls
  3. Make ready 1/3 cup cooked crumbled bacon
  4. Get 8 oz. cream cheese
  5. Take 2 tbsp butter
  6. Take 2 tbsp milk
  7. Get 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  8. Prepare to taste Salt & Pepper

Instructions to make Chicken and bacon stuffed croissants:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cook 2 chicken breast and shred (I use Kitchenaid mixer with paddle attachment)
  3. Add in remaining ingredients except crescent rolls and mix well
  4. Open both cans of crescent rolls and pinch together creases to create 8 separate rectangles.
  5. Place a generous amount of chicken and bacon mixture in the center of the crescent roll. Pull up corners diagonally and pinch together. Repeat with all 8 rectangles.
  6. Place on a baking sheet and bake 13-15 minutes until golden brown.
