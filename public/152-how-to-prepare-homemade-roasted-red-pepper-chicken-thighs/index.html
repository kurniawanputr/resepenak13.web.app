The ingredients needed to make Roasted Red Pepper Chicken 🐔 Thighs:
  1. Get 1 large pack chicken thighs (bone in, skin on)
  2. Make ready 2 onions, sliced
  3. Take 1 green pepper, sliced
  4. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  5. Get 1/4 teaspoon Goya adobo
  6. Get 6 cloves garlic, minced
  7. Get 1 can (15.5 ounce) Goya black beans, undrained
  8. Take 1 can (14.5 ounce) diced tomatoes, undrained
  9. Take 1 jar (11.5 ounce) Goya pimientos, (roasted red peppers) drained, cut into strips

Instructions to make Roasted Red Pepper Chicken 🐔 Thighs:
  1. Rinse and pat dry your chicken thighs…….
  2. Place thighs in a large baking pan…….
  3. Place your sliced onions and green pepper all around chicken thighs…….
  4. In a bowl combine: black pepper, adobo, minced garlic, Goya black beans and diced tomatoes……
  5. Pour black bean / diced tomato mixture evenly all over chicken thighs……
  6. Place your sliced Goya pimientos all over chicken thighs……
  7. Bake thighs, uncovered, in a preheated 400 degree oven for 1 hour…….Baste chicken thighs with drippings when done….. 🤩😋
  8. Serve with your favorite side dish and enjoy 😉!! I served mine with chicken flavored parsley white rice 🍚!!!
