The ingredients needed to make Red Beef Stroganoff:
  1. Take 1 stick salted butter, not margarine
  2. Take 1 lb stew beef, cut into bite size pieces
  3. Take 1 large yellow onion, cut onion in half then slice
  4. Take 2 regular or 1 large package of sliced mushrooms of your choice
  5. Prepare 2 cans any tomato soup, i.e. Campbells
  6. Take 1 can tomato paste
  7. Make ready 1 Cup sour cream
  8. Prepare Package Egg Noodles
  9. Get Couple of dashes of Worcestershire sauce
  10. Take Seasoning salt to your taste
  11. Prepare 1 \4 teaspoon Red pepper flakes optional
  12. Make ready 1 \8 teaspoon cayenne pepper sauce optional
