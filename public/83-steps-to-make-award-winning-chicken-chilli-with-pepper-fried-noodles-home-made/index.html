The ingredients needed to make Chicken chilli with pepper fried noodles (home-made):
  1. Prepare For the chilli chicken
  2. Get Chicken (boneless)
  3. Take 1/2 cup corn flour
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Make ready 1 onion diced
  6. Take 1/2 red/yellow bell peppers
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp soy sauce
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  9. Take 1 tbsp stir fry sauce
  10. Take For the noodles
  11. Get 1 packet noodles (chings hakka)
  12. Take Peppercorns
  13. Get 1 egg (scrambled)
  14. Prepare Vegetable oil
  15. Take Salt
