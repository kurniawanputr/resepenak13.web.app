The ingredients needed to make Chicken tinga:
  1. Get 3 Chicken breast
  2. Take 1 Red onion
  3. Get 2 Tomatoes
  4. Prepare 1 Can chipotle peppers
  5. Get 2 Garlic cloves
  6. Make ready Pinch salt
  7. Take Black Pepper seasoning
  8. Make ready Bag tostadas
  9. Make ready 2 Avocados
  10. Get Sour cream

Chicken Tinga, or tinga de pollo, is a Mexican dish consisting of shredded chicken in a saucy, smoky, spicy tomato chipotle sauce. It's not only quick and easy, but healthy.

Instructions to make Chicken tinga:
  1. Boil chicken breast & when done set aside for few minutes to cool down.
  2. Slice red onion while you wait for chicken to cool off. Add black pepper seasoning & pinch of salt.
  3. Add some oil in frying pan & add onions. Add pinch of salt & some black Pepper seasoning.
  4. Shred chicken when its cooled of.
  5. Add chicken onto fry pan with onions & stir. Add two slices of garlic cloves.
  6. Open can of chipotle peppers & add with two tomatoes in blender & mix. If you want it less spicy add more tomatoes. The more tomatoes the less spicy.
  7. When done add into frying tan & stir.
  8. Heat for 5 minutes.
  9. Serve onto tostadas with sour cream & enjoy.
  10. You can also make tinga with white onion if you want. I like red onions better. Add some avocados & enjoy.
