The ingredients needed to make Buffalo Chicken Nachos!!!!:
  1. Prepare 1/2 Rotisserie chicken (shredded, both dark and white meat)
  2. Take 2 tbs softened cream cheese
  3. Take 3 tbs franks red hot sauce
  4. Prepare 1 bag tortilla chips (Restaurant Style)*
  5. Get 1 cup salsa
  6. Get 1/2 large green bell pepper, diced
  7. Make ready 1/2 large yellow pepper, diced
  8. Take 1.5 cups shredded cheese (I used nacho cheese blend)
  9. Prepare slices To taste: sliced pickled jalepeno

Instructions to make Buffalo Chicken Nachos!!!!:
  1. In a bowl mix your shredded chicken with cream cheese and hot sauce
  2. In a large parchment lined baking sheet, or in a large oven safe dish. Layer your nachos
    1. chips 2) Dallops of chicken mixture 3) peppers 4) salsa, shredded cheese 5) picked jalepeno slices making sure to end with the cheese
  4. Set in 350°F oven for 25-30min or until cheese is melted and bubbly
  5. Serve with sour cream and additional salsa if desired
  6. I use Que Pasa Tortilla Chips as I feel they hold up better to loaded nachos.
