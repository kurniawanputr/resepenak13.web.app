The ingredients needed to make Beef matoke:
  1. Prepare 6 half-sliced potatoes
  2. Make ready 10 half-sliced bananas(matoke)
  3. Prepare 1 chopped onion
  4. Make ready 2 diced tomatoes
  5. Make ready 1 hot pepper
  6. Make ready Half crushed garlic
  7. Get 1/2 beef
  8. Get Coriander
  9. Make ready Cooking oil

  4. Add coriander then mix it up
