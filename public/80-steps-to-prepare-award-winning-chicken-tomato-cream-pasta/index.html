Steps to make Chicken Tomato Cream Pasta:
  1. Salt your chicken and place in a hot skillet with olive oil. You want a hot skillet so you get a nice crust on the chicken.
  2. Start to boil spaghetti. And once chicken is done cooking, place on cutting board to rest.
  3. Using the same skillet you used to cook the chicken, place tomatoes in pan with some extra olive oil. Then add the half and half and pesto. Let simmer.
  4. Once spaghetti is done place in cream sauce. Then cut chicken and add to pasta.
  5. Voilà! Enjoy!
