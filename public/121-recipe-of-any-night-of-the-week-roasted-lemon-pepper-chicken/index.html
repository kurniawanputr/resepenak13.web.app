The ingredients needed to make Roasted Lemon Pepper Chicken:
  1. Get 2 whole chickens
  2. Prepare to taste Lemon pepper seasoning
  3. Make ready to taste Black pepper
  4. Prepare to taste Sazon (sin achiote)
  5. Take to taste Oregano
  6. Make ready to taste White vinegar
  7. Get as needed Vegetable oil
  8. Take 10-20 cloves garlic, minced
  9. Prepare to taste Parsley flakes
