Steps to make General Tso Noodles with Chicken:
  1. Stir-fry chicken until medium cooked.
  2. Add veggies and continue stir-frying process until chicken and veggies are cooked (approximately 20 minutes, but varies).
  3. Add General Tso sauce and cook for another 5-10 minutes so it blends with chicken and veggies.
  4. Cook rice noodles separately (to your own taste)
  5. Add noodles to General Tso chicken sauce and toss several times.
