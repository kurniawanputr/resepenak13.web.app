The ingredients needed to make Naked Chicken Parmesan:
  1. Prepare 2 pounds chicken breast
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese shredded
  3. Make ready 2 cups mozzarella cheese shredded
  4. Make ready 15 ounces tomato sauce
  5. Get 1 teaspoons thyme
  6. Take 1 teaspoons salt
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  8. Get 1 teaspoons granulated onion powder
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoons granulated garlic powder

Instructions to make Naked Chicken Parmesan:
  1. Preheat the oven 400° Fahrenheit.
  2. Cut the chicken lengthwise, kinda like butterflying the chicken breast.
  3. Brown the chicken add the seasonings. 4 minutes per side.
  4. Add tomato sauce and cheese. Put the Parmesan on first then cover the whole top of chicken with mozzarella. Put into the oven for 20-25 minutes. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!
